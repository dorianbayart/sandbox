Minimalist Tower War

Minimalist Tower War is a strategy game where you command an army of units to capture towers and conquer the map.

Create paths from your towers to send units towards enemy towers and watch as your forces clash in a minimalist battle for dominance.

The first player to capture all towers wins !

Rules

  • Capture towers: Drag from your towers to ennemy ones to create a path and send units to capture it.
  • Units attack: Units move along paths and automatically attack enemy towers, decreasing their level.
  • Tower levels: Once a tower's level reaches zero, it's captured and joins your side.
  • Increase tower level: Send units to your own towers to increase their level.
  • Multiple paths: Higher-level towers allow for more paths, enabling you to launch attacks from multiple directions.
  • Strategic path creation: Create paths strategically to outmaneuver your opponents and capture their towers.
  • Dominate the map: Be the first to capture all towers and claim victory in Minimalist Tower War!
